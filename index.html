About Us

A FEW WORDS ABOUT US

Incorporated in the year 2013, we “Tikks Industries” are engaged in manufacturing a superior quality range of Mayonnaise, Tomato Ketchup, Snack Sauce, Dark Soya Sauce, Red Chilli Sauce, Green Chilli Sauce, etc. These products are appreciated for their purity, longer shelf life and delicious taste.

Under the leadership of our mentor “Mr. Vikramjit Singh”, we have been able to gain tremendous success in this domain.

OUR PRODUCT ITEMS

With the assistance of our advanced production unit, we are occupied in manufacturing an optimum quality range of Mayonnaise, Tomato Ketchup, Snack Sauce, Dark Soya Sauce, Red Chilli Sauce, Green Chilli Sauce, etc. These products are prepared hygienically in our production unit by making use of high-quality ingredients, natural flavors, and preservatives. Our offered range of products is highly appreciated among our clients for purity, rich taste, high nutritional value, and longer shelf life. Besides, we provide our product range at affordable prices.
